HOW TO PLAY

1. Choose either 'Play VR Experience' or 'Play Non-VR Experience'
	- Please choose 'Play VR Experience' if you own a VR headset 
 
2. Read the instructions on the Instruction Panel in front of you before playing

3. The game will automatically close when the 10 minute timer has run out
	- The timer will not start until the door has been opened

4. Once the game has closed, please fill out the survey attached to the email

5. Please send the completed survey to myself, Alex, at 40312219@live.napier.ac.uk

6. You may uninstall the game when the survey is complete
